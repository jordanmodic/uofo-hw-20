Day Findr

Connect with your destination.

Porject Overview

Every traveler wants to have a unique, authentic experience at their destination. This goal calls many of us to travel in the first place. Discovering the spaces and experiences that make each destination one of a kind. As people travel more and more frequently, individuals are looking to be more than a tourist. They’re looking for experiences that make them a traveler.

The Solution

Provide travelers with a tool for finding customized, culturally representative day itineraries and activities. Day Findr incorporates users interests and local highlights to offer tailored travel offerings. Through creating customized itineraries and suggestions, Day Findr saves travelers time and ensures they discover authentic experiences.

The Problem

Travelers find it challenging to plan full day itineraries while sourcing authentic, cultural experiences at their destination. This planning process is time consuming and travelers lack to appropriate resources for finding these experiences.

User Insight

A busy traveler who is passionate about building cultural understanding through authentic connections needs a tool for efficiently finding activities tailored to this interest. The current planning process can be stressful and inefficient.


Problem Statement

Busy travelers who are passionate about building cultural understanding through authentic, culturally immersive experiences find it difficult to research, plan and budget for these activities. How might we develop tools that help connect travelers to these itineraries at their destination?

Final Thoughts

Identifying the Problem

I began the research phase of the product believing that many travelers struggled to budget for activities at their destination and as a result often overspent. Through my research I found that travelers were more concerned with sourcing authentic, unique experiences than going over budget. This discovery reframed my focus to these types of activities and resulted in a whole new area of travel planning to explore.

Honing the Features

Throughout the iteration and prototyping process I found myself honing and pairing down the features I wanted Day Findr to offer. It was easy to begin the process with a long list of interesting the exciting features to design. Through a combination of competitor analysis and instructor feedback I realized the need modify my design to meet an unserved corner of the travel market.

Cyclical Process

This Case Study was a great first learning experience in the cyclical process. Iterating, testing, and investigating the results helped me in further understanding the needs of users as well as best design practices. I am eager to continue this learning process through iterating on these designs as well as future design projects.
